PDF Downloads

PDFs are saved via Chrome's download dialog. To make it always open to your vault's PDF folder:

1. Open Chrome Settings → Downloads
2. Turn on "Ask where to save each file"
3. The first time you save a PDF, navigate to your vault PDF folder — Chrome will remember it from then on.
